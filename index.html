ABOUT US

Lock It In! is an innovative financing solution designed to help potential buyers and sellers navigate high interest rates.  With our novel 3x3 financing plan, potential buyers can secure a 3-year lease payment at a 3% interest rate in exchange for an upfront Lock It In! payment of 20% or more of the home's value (as negotiated).  After 3 years the remaining balance of the property is paid in exchange for title to the home (otherwise the owner must return the upfront Lock It In! payment plus home price appreciation).   Lock It In! financing closes in weeks, defers taxes, has lower fees and provides potential buyers and sellers with a win-win outcome!

WHAT WE OFFER

Lock It In! offers a flexible 3x3 Alternative Financing plan designed to benefit both potential buyers and sellers.

Lessors, you receive a negotiated 25% upfront payment from the Lessee, which can act as a down payment or investment. In return, you provide a
3-year lease with payments based on a 3.00% loan rate, covering 75% of the property’s value. At lease end, you can either sell the property or retain it by repaying the initial payment with any property appreciation.

Lessees, this plan allows you to invest in the property with a 25% upfront payment and make lease payments that are 40-60% lower than typical mortgage rates. After 3 years, you can either move out and reclaim your investment with a share of the appreciation, or purchase the property by paying the remaining balance and gain full ownership.
